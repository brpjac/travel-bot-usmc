# DTS, DTS-R, and Claims

**DTS is a travel management system, not an orders type** [C014]. Which system
you touch depends on who you are and what's being booked.

## Who uses what

| You are | System | For |
|---|---|---|
| Active Reserve / Active Duty | **DTS** | All travel — standard process [C014] |
| SMCR on HTC IDT | **DTS-R** | The $750 travel voucher, NLT 15 days prior [C002] |
| SMCR needing commercial transportation outside DTS | **T3** | The only method of reserving commercial transportation for reservists outside DTS [C019] |
| SMCR on AT/ADOS orders | DTS (when authorized for the event) or T3 | Depends on the event — see below |

(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026; ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 1)

## DTS vs T3 for reservists

The ForO's scope rule: the T3 SOP does **not** apply to Reserve and Active
Component/Active Reserve Marines using DTS to schedule travel for a
**non-TEEP-funded event**. (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 5 admin provisions.) In
practice: TEEP-funded training events run through T3; if DTS is authorized for
your movement, DTS handles booking and vouchering. When in doubt, ask the S-1
which system your event uses BEFORE the T3 clock
([t3-timelines.md](t3-timelines.md)) runs out.

## Orders vs booking vs reimbursement

Three different things, three different systems:

1. **Orders** — written in MROWS or DTS ([mrows-dts-orders.md](mrows-dts-orders.md))
2. **Booking transportation** — T3/TOP for commercial transport outside DTS
   ([t3-overview.md](t3-overview.md))
3. **Reimbursement** — DTS/DTS-R vouchers after travel
   ([../entitlements/idt-travel-reimbursement.md](../entitlements/idt-travel-reimbursement.md))
