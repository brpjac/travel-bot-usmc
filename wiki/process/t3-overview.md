# T3 Overview

**T3** = TEEP, Transportation of Personnel, Transportation of Things — the
MARFORRES program and web database that plans, funds, and books training
travel. The load-bearing fact: **T3 is the only method of reserving commercial
transportation for reservists outside of DTS** [C019] (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 1,
p. 1-1).

## The three letters

- **TEEP** — Training Exercise and Employment Plan: the funded training
  schedule. Events are either TEEP-funded or Non-TEEP-funded
  ([teep-planning.md](teep-planning.md)).
- **TOP** — Transportation of Personnel: your flight/bus/charter request,
  actioned by DMO ([t3-timelines.md](t3-timelines.md)).
- **TOT** — Transportation of Things: equipment and supply shipments.

## Do I need a T3 request?

- SMCR traveler funded by TEEP/Non-TEEP dollars, needing DMO to schedule
  travel → **yes**, an approved T3 request generates the TOP request.
  (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 5, p. 5-3)
- Reserve Marine using DTS for a Non-TEEP funded event → **no** T3 request.
  (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 5, p. 5-3)
- AD/AR Marine on DTS without TEEP funding → **no** T3 request.
- Booking your own flight through a website, travel agency, or airport
  counter → **never authorized**; travel goes through the servicing Travel
  Management Company [C040] (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 5, p. 5-3).

More detail on the DTS/T3 split: [dts-and-claims.md](dts-and-claims.md).

## Who's who

- **MARFORRES/MSC T3 Manager** — final approver of T3 requests; creates T3
  events.
- **DMO** (Distribution Management Office) — receives TOP/TOT requests, makes
  reservations, tickets travelers.
- **MSC G-3/5 and Chief of Staff** — the endorsement chain when timelines are
  missed ([t3-waivers.md](t3-waivers.md)).

## Access

T3 is a CAC-gated web application (https://t3.mcdsus.mcds.usmc.mil/);
permissions are assigned by the MARFORRES or MSC T3 Managers. Training videos
live in the "T3 Video Training" tab. (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 6, p. 6-1.) Full
text: [../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch6-t3-database.md](../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch6-t3-database.md).
