# Non-TEEP Funding

Events that don't qualify for the TEEP still move people and gear — on
separate money, tracked in T3 [C031]. Source: ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 4 (verbatim:
[../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch4-non-teep-funding.md](../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch4-non-teep-funding.md)).

## OCO (1106 O&M) — mobilization money (Ch 4, para 2, p. 4-1)

OSD designates which overseas operations qualify. Covers travel, per diem, and
supplies for activated/mobilized MARFORRES personnel (activations generally
under 10 U.S.C. §12302). Identified on the "Mobilization Funding" line of the
TEEP request's Expense Categories page.

## Schools funding (1108 RPMC) (Ch 4, para 3, p. 4-1)

- **RPT** (Refresher and Proficiency Training): the primary source for formal
  schools — pay/allowances, travel, per diem. PID RPT in MROWS.
- **CDT** (Career Development Training): PME/RPME courses required for
  promotion eligibility. PID CDT in MROWS.

## Other Non-TEEP RPMC lines (Ch 4, para 4, p. 4-2)

Pay/allowances, travel and per diem for reservists (unless DTS is used):

| Purpose | MROWS PID |
|---|---|
| Legal/Court Martial (COMMARFORRES ordered) | CM |
| Funeral Honors | G |
| Marksmanship | H |
| Service or Mission Support | F |
| Global War on Terrorism | GWT |
| Inspections | INS |
| Boards and Conferences | J |
| Operational Training | Q |

## O&MMCR 1107 — non-TEEP things (Ch 4, para 5, p. 4-2)

TOT for non-TEEP requirements: equipment redistribution, maintenance not tied
to TEEP exercises, and the Small Package Program (FedEx/UPS/DHL). Small
packages have their own 7-day DMO deadline [C035] —
[t3-timelines.md](t3-timelines.md).
