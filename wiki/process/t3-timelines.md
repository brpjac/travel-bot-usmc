# T3 Submission Timelines

The #1 travel failure mode is submitting late. Work backwards from departure
day **D**. All timelines from ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 5 (verbatim text:
[../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch5-t3-logistics.md](../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch5-t3-logistics.md)).

## TOP ladder — non-SAAM flights (Ch 5, para 3.i, p. 5-6)

| Deadline | What must have happened | Claim |
|---|---|---|
| **D-65** | Group charter T3 request received by final approver (MARFORRES/MSC T3 Manager) | [C020] |
| **D-60** | Group charter TOP request received by DMO | [C021] |
| **D-50** | OCONUS commercial air T3 request (individual/group) received by final approver | [C022] |
| **D-45** | OCONUS commercial air TOP request received by DMO with approved/authenticated orders | [C023] |
| **D-35** | CONUS commercial air/bus TOP request received by MARFORRES/MSC T3 Manager | [C024] |
| **D-30** | CONUS commercial air/bus TOP request received by DMO with authenticated orders | [C025] |
| **D-10** | GTCC IBA activated; authenticated orders attached to the TOP request or DMO cancels it. Ticketing begins | [C026] |
| **D-5** | DMO ticketing complete | [C027] |

Summary of DMO deadlines [C032]: **CONUS TOP 30 days, OCONUS TOP 45 days**
(from the ladder above); TOT: **CONUS 30, OCONUS 60, Small Package Program 7**
[C035] (Ch 5, para 2.c, p. 5-1).

## The 15-day tripwire [C034]

If orders are not approved by **15 days prior to departure**, reservations are
**cancelled** and the unit must resubmit with an MSC Chief of Staff waiver.
Any new submission inside 15 days also requires the CoS waiver justifying the
late submission. (Ch 5, para 3.f(5), p. 5-5.) Missed a deadline? →
[t3-waivers.md](t3-waivers.md).

## Practical sequencing for a Marine

1. Orders first: the T3 request needs DTS/MROWS orders generated, and the TOP
   needs **authenticated** orders before ticketing (Ch 5, para 3.f). MIU
   orders lead times: [orders-request-miu.md](orders-request-miu.md).
2. Reservations are made 5-10 days after DMO receives the TOP; ticketing
   happens within 10 days of travel against the CBA — your **IBA must be
   active NLT 10 days out** or you risk cancellation [C036]
   (Ch 5, para 3.g, p. 5-5).
3. Changes cost time and money: additions to a manifest require a NEW T3
   request; drops/cancels are simple remarks (Ch 5, para 3.h).

## Worked example

OCONUS individual commercial air, departing 1 October: T3 request to final
approver by ~12 August (D-50) [C022], TOP at DMO by ~17 August (D-45) [C023],
orders authenticated and GTCC active by 21 September (D-10) [C026]. The MIU
orders request itself was due 60 days out (~2 August) —
[orders-request-miu.md](orders-request-miu.md).
