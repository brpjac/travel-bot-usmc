# TEEP Funding Lines

Two major appropriations fund Reserve training: **RPMC (1108)** and **O&MMCR
(1107)**, plus Joint Exercise Program and IRT money for specific event types
[C030]. Source: ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 3 (verbatim:
[../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch3-teep-funding.md](../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch3-teep-funding.md)).

## RPMC 1108 — people money (Ch 3, para 2, pp. 3-1 to 3-2)

- **TEEP ADOS (2732)**: pay/allowances, per diem, and travel for reservists;
  PID 3 in MROWS. ADOS orders use DTS for travel but still need a T3 TEEP
  request for funding.
- **Reserve Travel (2731)**: AT travel and per diem (PID 1), group commercial
  travel (2-99 pax), commercial charter (100+), contracted AT billeting,
  **offsite IDT travel and per diem (PID 2)** — with the catch that the
  offsite drill must be in direct support of an approved TEEP funded event
  [C039] — and chartered drill-weekend buses.

## O&MMCR 1107 — things and support money (Ch 3, para 3, pp. 3-2 to 3-3)

- **Exercise Support**: consumables/"Bill of Material" and services for
  approved exercises or unit ATs. Not for drill weekends unless in direct
  support of an approved TEEP exercise (Ch 5, para 5.e, p. 5-7).
- **TAD**: travel/per diem for AC/AR Marines (and Navy personnel assigned to
  reserve units) supporting TEEP events.
- **TOT**: moving equipment/supplies — must be T3-approved before shipment.
- **PEM**: post-exercise equipment repair (pre/post LTIs in GCSS-MC; post-LTI
  within 30 days).

## Joint Exercise Program (0100) — CJCS exercises only (Ch 3, para 4, pp. 3-3 to 3-4)

- **CTP**: the airline ticket only (no per diem), unit must be on the TPFDD.
- **SIF**: travel/per diem for CJCS planning conferences and host-nation
  exercise support. Never mix TEEP Exercise Support funds into a
  SIF-appropriate bill — the unit eats the cost.

## IRT and AC ADOS (Ch 3, paras 5-6, pp. 3-4 to 3-5)

IRT O&M funds TAD/TOT/exercise support for IRT projects and long-term project
manager ADOS (30-365 days, PID IRT). Active-Component ADOS money (MPMC) pays
reservists directly supporting the AC, with AC O&M covering travel via DTS.

## AT day-count rules [C038] (Ch 3, para 7, p. 3-5)

- Standard AT: **14 days + 1 travel day = 15 days**.
- **Extended AT**: up to **29 days** total with COMMARFORRES G-3/5 approval.
- **Split AT**: two periods totaling ≤15 days; MSC G3s approve; an AT may only
  be split **once**.
- **Additional AT**: a second 15-day AT; both ATs together ≤**30 days**;
  extended AT days can't be converted into an additional AT.
- Requests go via MSC Commanders to COMMARFORRES through AMHS with
  justification, headcount, days per Marine, and impact statement.
