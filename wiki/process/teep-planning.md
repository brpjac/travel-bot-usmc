# TEEP Planning: TEEP vs Non-TEEP Events

Every T3 event is one of two kinds: **TEEP funded** (part of the funded
training plan) or **Non-TEEP funded** (tracked in T3, funded from separate
lines). Which kind your event is determines the money and the paperwork.
Source: ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 2 (verbatim:
[../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch2-teep-development.md](../sources/foro-3000-52-1/ch2-teep-development.md)).

## What qualifies as a TEEP event (Ch 2, para 4, p. 2-2)

MARFORRES sponsored events (e.g. ITX), Joint Exercise Program events, joint
events with other Services, OPTEMPO-relief events for active units, integrated
AC/RC training, Innovative Readiness Training, Deployment-for-Training OCONUS,
MSC directed exercises, exercise-related unit training, and conferences
supporting those events. **Individual skills, licensing courses and training
symposiums do not qualify as TEEP events.**

## What's Non-TEEP (Ch 2, para 7, p. 2-3)

Schools and specialized training, PME, equipment redistribution, Small Package
Program shipments, administrative TAD, and Band movements — tracked in T3 for
transportation accountability, funded separately
([non-teep-funding.md](non-teep-funding.md)).

## How events get sourced (Ch 2, paras 2-3, p. 2-1)

Exercises originate in the Global Force Management process via quarterly Force
Synchronization Conferences; MARFORRES planners capture requirements in T3.
Priority: augmentation/reinforcement of active-component units first, then
other GFM activations, then exercises (CJCS → ITX-Reserve → other SLTEs →
bilateral). Non-TEEP requirements are coordinated internally at MSC/MARFORRES
TEEP Conferences.

## The budgeting rhythm (Ch 2, paras 5 & 8, pp. 2-2 to 2-4)

Annual TEEP conference identifies next-FY requirements → Comptroller publishes
fiscal guidance in July/August → the G-3/5 T3 Manager builds the TEEP Spend
Plan (by funding category, quarter, MSC) → MSCs prioritize within their
controls → sourced units estimate funds by fiscal category (ADOS, TAD, TOT,
TOP) and submit a TEEP request in T3 → the TEEP approval chain releases funds
for execution.

## Why a Marine should care

Your travel is only as funded as the event behind it. If the event isn't in
the TEEP (or registered as Non-TEEP), there is no money for the TOP request —
and no waiver fixes that. Confirm the event's TEEP number and SIC with your
S-3 before planning travel ([mrows-dts-orders.md](mrows-dts-orders.md)).
