# Lodging

## By orders type

- **IDT**: lodging only at the HTC (on-base billeting if available) [C010].
  Submit a Billeting Request Form with the S-4 [C016].
- **Offsite IDT**: lodging if authorized on your orders.
- **AT / ADOS**: yes — full TDY entitlements [C013], commercial lodging at the
  per diem locality rate ([per-diem.md](per-diem.md)).

(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

## The IDT exception [C010]

The CO or Authorizing Official **can** authorize IDT lodging with a written
travel order when both are true:

1. The overnight stay is mission-required, and
2. The duty period exceeds 12 hours.

This is an exception, not an entitlement — it requires the written order before
the stay, not a reimbursement request after.

## Longer-distance IDT

Drilling 150+ miles away (with billet and BIC eligibility met) may rate
actual-expense reimbursement up to **$750 per round trip** [C041]
(MARADMIN 157/25, para 5). See
[idt-travel-reimbursement.md](idt-travel-reimbursement.md).
