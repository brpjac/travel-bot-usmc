# Entitlements by Orders Type

The core table. (MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

| Entitlement | IDT | Offsite IDT | AT | ADOS |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Per diem | **No** [C001] | Yes (M&IE) [C012] | Yes | Yes |
| Lodging | HTC only¹ | If authorized on orders | Yes | Yes |
| Rental car | **No** | Mission-required + AO approval only | If advantageous to the Government | Yes when authorized — most common [C011] |
| POV mileage | $750 voucher² | Standard rate | Standard rate | Standard rate |
| Commercial travel | **No** | — | Yes (air/rail if distance warrants) | Yes |
| Full TDY entitlements | No | Partial | **Yes** [C013] | **Yes** [C013] |

¹ CO/AO exception possible — see [lodging.md](lodging.md) [C010].
² Via DTS-R, NLT 15 days prior — see [idt-travel-reimbursement.md](idt-travel-reimbursement.md).

## Rules of thumb

- Need a rental car? You almost certainly want **ADOS** [C011] —
  [../orders-types/ados.md](../orders-types/ados.md).
- On IDT and hoping for per diem: it doesn't exist on IDT [C001]. Change the
  orders type, not the expectation.
- "Full TDY entitlements" (AT/ADOS) means the JTR TDY rules apply: per diem
  ([per-diem.md](per-diem.md)), lodging, transportation, and the POV
  constructed-travel rules ([pov-mileage.md](pov-mileage.md)).
