# IDT Travel Reimbursement

IDT carries no per diem — but the **IDT Travel Reimbursement (IDT-R) program**
reimburses select Marines who commute a long way to drill. The current rules
are MARADMIN 157/25 (24 Mar 2025), which raised the cap from $500 to **$750
per round trip** [C041]. Full message:
[../sources/maradmin-157-25/message.md](../sources/maradmin-157-25/message.md).

## The $750 cap [C041]

Actual costs between your primary residence (as entered in MCTFS) and your
Reserve Training Center, up to **$750 per round trip** — for round trips
ending on or after **27 December 2024**. (Round trips that ended earlier fall
under the old $500 cap.) Reimbursable: commercial transportation including
home↔terminal legs, POV mileage at the "other mileage rate" plus parking and
tolls, rental car travel-day costs, and actual meal costs up to the locality
M&IE rate. Keep receipts — required for any single expense of $75 or more.
(MARADMIN 157/25, para 5.a.)

This is what the MIU matrix's "$750 travel voucher" implements — submit it
**NLT 15 days prior to the trip** per unit procedure [C002]
(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026).

## Are you eligible? [C042]

All three gates must pass (MARADMIN 157/25, para 3):

1. **Billet**: critical staffing shortfall within the SMCR, an SMCR
   command-screened billet, a DC M&RA Senior Leader/General Officer
   assignment, or an IMA RSLB billet. (IMA Marines outside GO/RSLB billets
   are NOT eligible.)
2. **Distance**: your unit is **150+ miles** from your official primary
   residence in MCTFS, by DTOD (PCS/TDY route type).
3. **BIC match**: assigned to a BIC matching your PMOS/AMOS (one-up/down
   grade OK) or holding an RA-approved exception.

No rank or MOS limitations otherwise. Waivers go via NAVMC 10274 through the
chain of command to COMMARFORRES AC/S G-1 (para 4). MARFORRES publishes a
**monthly eligibility roster** — being on it (or holding a waiver) is what
your unit verifies before you claim [C045].

## Limits and mechanics

- **11 round trips per FY** (22 for MOS 75XX DIFOP aviators) [C043].
- Claims settle in **DTS** against the FY Line of Accounting — MARFORRES adds
  eligible Marines to a restricted cross-org DTS list; settle outstanding
  claims NLT 31 December of the same calendar year [C045]. Questions:
  SMB_IDT_TRAVEL@USMC.MIL
  ([../reference/contacts-and-systems.md](../reference/contacts-and-systems.md)).
- **Not combinable with offsite IDT travel** — IDT-R (long commute to your
  OWN unit) and off-site IDT travel funding are separate authorities [C044].
- With a GTCC you can book **City Pair Program** fares through the CTO
  (CI Travel) using the IDT travel form — refundable tickets, no change fees.
  (MARADMIN 157/25, para 7.)
- A commander **cannot deny** a request that meets program eligibility [C046].

## What IDT reimbursement is NOT

- Not per diem — there is no daily rate on IDT [C001].
- Not automatic at 150 miles — the billet and BIC gates are real [C042].
- Not transportation money at the drill site — no authority exists to
  reimburse getting around AT the IDT location (MARADMIN 157/25, para 5.a).
