# Source Documents

What feeds this wiki, and when to go deeper. Machine-readable registry:
[../_sources.yml](../_sources.yml).

## Joint Travel Regulations (JTR) — Jul 2026 copy

The DoD-wide travel entitlements regulation (per diem, transportation, POV,
lodging). 572 pages; only the reserve-relevant paragraphs are transcribed
in [../sources/jtr/](../sources/CLAUDE.md). The bot can also search the full
raw text. Updates monthly — refreshed 19 Jul 2026; page-number citations are
edition-specific ([precedence-and-currency.md](precedence-and-currency.md)).

## MCO 1001R.1L (MCRAMM) — Dec 2015 w/ Admin CH-2

The Marine Corps Reserve Administrative Management Manual: reserve
participation, duty types, administrative machinery. This is the revision
ForO 3000-52.1 references; it replaced our 2009 .1K copy on 19 Jul 2026
(searchable via the bot's deep-search fallback). No chapters transcribed yet —
[../orders-types/mobilization.md](../orders-types/mobilization.md) is the
first candidate.

## ForO 3000-52.1 — MARFORRES TEEP/T3 SOP, Apr 2019

The reserve travel-booking bible: TEEP events, funding lines, T3 database,
TOP/TOT submission timelines, waiver process, MROWS/DTS order rules.
Distribution Statement A. The original is a scan with no text layer — the
transcriptions in
[../sources/foro-3000-52-1/](../sources/foro-3000-52-1/basic-order.md) are its
only searchable form.

## MIU Orders-Type Matrix — 6 Mar 2026

Unit guidance: which orders type for which trip, MIU submission lead times,
prerequisites, entitlement summaries. Distribution-marked, so the document
itself lives outside the repo; its mechanics are distilled into the
[orders-types](../orders-types/CLAUDE.md) and
[process](../process/CLAUDE.md) pages.

## MARADMIN 157/25 — IDT Travel Reimbursement Update, 24 Mar 2025

The current IDT-R program guidance: raises the reimbursement cap from $500 to
$750 per round trip (round trips ending on/after 27 Dec 2024), sets the
billet/distance/BIC eligibility gates, the 11-trip FY limit, and the DTS
claim process; cancels MARADMIN 193/22. Publicly released on marines.mil; the
raw PDF is an archival capture generated from the message text (captured
18 Jul 2026), and the verbatim transcript lives in
[../sources/maradmin-157-25/message.md](../sources/maradmin-157-25/message.md).
