# Acronyms

## Orders and duty types

| Acronym | Meaning |
|---|---|
| IDT | Inactive Duty Training — standard drill periods |
| AT | Annual Training — typically 12-17 day training periods |
| ADOS | Active Duty Operational Support — short-term active duty |
| SMCR | Selected Marine Corps Reserve |
| AR | Active Reserve |
| TDY | Temporary Duty |

## Places and thresholds

| Acronym | Meaning |
|---|---|
| HTC | Home Training Center — where a Marine is assigned to drill |
| CONUS | Continental United States (48 contiguous states + DC) |
| OCONUS | Outside Continental United States (AK, HI, territories, overseas) |
