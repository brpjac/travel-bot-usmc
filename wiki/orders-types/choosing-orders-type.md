# Choosing Your Orders Type

Your orders type is determined by three questions: **what's your service type,
where are you going, and how long is the trip?** (MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

## Step 1 — Service type

- **Active Reserve or Active Duty**: stop here. All your travel runs through
  standard DTS — DTS is the travel management system, not an orders type [C014].
  See [../process/dts-and-claims.md](../process/dts-and-claims.md).
- **SMCR**: continue below.

## Step 2 — Destination (SMCR)

- **Your HTC** → **IDT**. Standard drills, no travel entitlements. See [idt.md](idt.md).
- **More than 50 miles from your HTC** → you need travel-bearing orders:
  Offsite IDT, AT, or ADOS [C003]. Continue to Step 3.
- **OCONUS** → same three options, longer lead times, plus APACS.

## Step 3 — Duration and need (>50 mi trips)

| Trip | Orders type | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Drill periods away from HTC | **Offsite IDT** — [offsite-idt.md](offsite-idt.md) | IDT pay + travel entitlements |
| Longer than 7 days | **AT** — [at.md](at.md) | Annual training, full TDY entitlements [C008] |
| Shorter than 7 days | **ADOS** — [ados.md](ados.md) | Full TDY entitlements incl. rental car [C008] |

## The under-50-miles trap

Under 50 miles from HTC, the default is IDT — which carries **no** lodging,
rental car, or per diem. If you genuinely need those, your options are [C015]:

1. Request a CO exception for IDT lodging (mission-required overnight, duty
   period over 12 hours — see [../entitlements/lodging.md](../entitlements/lodging.md)), or
2. Request **ADOS** orders, which carry full TDY entitlements.

## Submission deadlines (don't skip this)

| Destination | Orders type | Submit by |
|---|---|---|
| HTC | IDT | 15 days prior ($750 voucher in DTS-R) |
| >50 mi CONUS | Offsite IDT / AT | 45 days prior |
| >50 mi CONUS | ADOS | 2 weeks prior |
| OCONUS | Offsite IDT / AT | 60 days prior |
| OCONUS | ADOS | 3 weeks prior |

(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026.) Full workflow and prerequisites:
[../process/orders-request-miu.md](../process/orders-request-miu.md). If
commercial air is involved, the T3 clock is longer than the orders clock —
check [../process/t3-timelines.md](../process/t3-timelines.md) first.
