# AT (Annual Training)

Annual Training is the yearly active-duty training period for SMCR Marines —
typically 12-17 days. Use AT orders for trips **longer than 7 days** [C008].
(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

## What it rates [C013]

Full TDY entitlements:

- Per diem: yes
- Lodging: yes
- Commercial travel: yes (air/rail if distance warrants)
- Rental car: may be authorized if advantageous to the Government

See [../entitlements/entitlements-matrix.md](../entitlements/entitlements-matrix.md).

## How to request (MIU)

Orders Request Form in the Correspondence Tracker:

- **CONUS**: 45 days prior [C004]
- **OCONUS**: 60 days prior + APACS [C006]

Prerequisites: current Medical, GTCC, Primary Residence [C009]. Workflow:
[../process/orders-request-miu.md](../process/orders-request-miu.md).

## Variants and funding

Extended AT, split AT, and additional AT are funded as TEEP lines — see
[../process/teep-funding.md](../process/teep-funding.md) (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 3).
If the AT event involves commercial air, T3/TOP submission deadlines apply on
top of the orders timeline:
[../process/t3-timelines.md](../process/t3-timelines.md).
