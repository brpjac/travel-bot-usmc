# IDT (Inactive Duty Training)

IDT is the standard drill period — what most SMCR Marines do at their Home
Training Center (HTC). Drills are conducted for pay on federal installations.
(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

## What IDT does NOT give you

No per diem, no rental car, no commercial travel reimbursement [C001]. IDT is
not a travel status. If your drill genuinely requires travel support, you're
looking at [offsite-idt.md](offsite-idt.md) or
[ados.md](ados.md) — see [choosing-orders-type.md](choosing-orders-type.md).

## What you DO get

- **Travel voucher**: up to $750 via DTS-R — submit NLT 15 days prior to the
  trip [C002]. Details:
  [../entitlements/idt-travel-reimbursement.md](../entitlements/idt-travel-reimbursement.md).
- **Lodging at the HTC only** (on-base billeting if available). The CO or
  Authorizing Official can authorize lodging with a written travel order when
  the overnight stay is mission-required and the duty period exceeds 12 hours
  [C010]. See [../entitlements/lodging.md](../entitlements/lodging.md).

## HTC drill checklist (MIU)

1. Submit the $750 travel voucher in DTS-R — NLT 15 days prior [C002].
2. Lodging needed? Billeting Request Form with the S-4 [C016].
3. Notify MIU_Operations@usmc.mil NLT 1 week prior to travel [C016].

## Related drill mechanisms

- **Courtesy drills**: coordinate with DET leadership; submitted in RTAMMS as
  courtesy unit musters. (MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)
- **Appropriate Duty**: NAVMC 799 to DET leadership via the MIU Drill
  Submission Page, uploaded into Drill Manager. (MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)
