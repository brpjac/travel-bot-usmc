# Mobilization / Activation

**Status: draft.** Our loaded sources (MCO 1001R.1L MCRAMM and ForO 3000-52.1)
cover this only at the margins. Treat this page as orientation, not guidance —
talk to your S-1 for anything real.

## What the loaded sources do say

MARFORRES units and individuals activated in support of contingency operations,
combatant commander security cooperation deployments, or disaster/emergency
response must submit transportation requests via T3 — unless DTS is authorized
for the movement — prior to transfer to, and upon return from, the Gaining
Force Command (GFC). Once OPCON/ADCON to the GFC, Marines fall under the GFC's
SOPs and directives. Preplanned and rotational activations follow the standard
T3 timelines; emergent requirements are handled as required for mission
accomplishment. (ForO 3000-52.1, Ch 5 admin/logistics provisions)
