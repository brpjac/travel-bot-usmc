# Offsite IDT

Offsite IDT is drilling somewhere other than your HTC — specifically, more than
50 miles from it [C003]. Unlike standard IDT, it comes with orders that can
carry travel entitlements. (MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

## When it applies

You're performing drill periods (not active duty) at a location >50 miles from
your HTC, CONUS or OCONUS. For duty rather than drills, compare
[at.md](at.md) / [ados.md](ados.md) via
[choosing-orders-type.md](choosing-orders-type.md).

Don't confuse this with **IDT Travel Reimbursement** (living 150+ miles from
your own unit and commuting to drill) — that's a separate authority and the
two may not be combined [C044] (MARADMIN 157/25, para 6.g). See
[../entitlements/idt-travel-reimbursement.md](../entitlements/idt-travel-reimbursement.md).

## What it rates [C012]

- **Per diem**: yes (meals and incidentals)
- **Lodging**: yes, if authorized on your orders
- **POV mileage**: yes, at the standard rate
- **Rental car**: only if mission-required AND approved by the Authorizing
  Official — not automatic

Details: [../entitlements/entitlements-matrix.md](../entitlements/entitlements-matrix.md).

## How to request (MIU)

Submit the Orders Request Form in the Correspondence Tracker:

- **CONUS**: 45 days prior to travel [C004]
- **OCONUS**: 60 days prior, and you also need APACS [C006]

You must be current on Medical, GTCC, and Primary Residence [C009]. Full
workflow: [../process/orders-request-miu.md](../process/orders-request-miu.md).

If your offsite drill involves commercial air, the T3/TOP clock runs longer
than the orders clock — see
[../process/t3-timelines.md](../process/t3-timelines.md).
