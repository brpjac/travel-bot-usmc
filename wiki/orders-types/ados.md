# ADOS (Active Duty Operational Support)

ADOS is short-term active duty support. Use ADOS orders for trips **shorter
than 7 days** [C008]. It's often the best option when a Marine needs real
travel entitlements — lodging, rental car, per diem — for a short trip [C011].
(MIU Orders-Type Matrix, 6 Mar 2026)

## What it rates [C013]

Full TDY entitlements:

- Per diem: yes
- Lodging: yes
- Rental car: yes when authorized — **ADOS is the most common orders type
  carrying rental-car entitlement** [C011]
- Commercial travel: yes

## When ADOS beats the alternatives

- Your duty is under 50 miles from the HTC but you need lodging or a rental
  car: IDT can't provide them; ADOS can. See the under-50-miles trap in
  [choosing-orders-type.md](choosing-orders-type.md).
- Short-fuse tasking: ADOS has the shortest lead time of any orders type.

## How to request (MIU)

Orders Request Form in the Correspondence Tracker:

- **CONUS**: NLT 2 weeks prior [C005]
- **OCONUS**: NLT 3 weeks prior [C007]

Prerequisites: current Medical, GTCC, Primary Residence (+APACS OCONUS) [C009].
Workflow: [../process/orders-request-miu.md](../process/orders-request-miu.md).
Note: 2 weeks covers the orders — commercial air still needs the T3/TOP ladder,
which is much longer. Check
[../process/t3-timelines.md](../process/t3-timelines.md) before assuming a
short-fuse trip is bookable.
