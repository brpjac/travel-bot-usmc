# ForO 3000-52.1 — Basic Order (verbatim)

UNITED STATES MARINE CORPS, Marine Forces Reserve, 2000 Opelousas Avenue,
New Orleans Louisiana 70114-1500. ForO 3000-52.1, G-3/5, APR 24 2019.

FORCE ORDER 3000-52.1

From: Commander — To: Distribution List

Subj: TRAINING EXERCISE AND EMPLOYMENT PLAN, TRANSPORTATION OF PERSONNEL,
TRANSPORTATION OF THINGS STANDING OPERATING PROCEDURES

Ref: (a) MCO 7300.21B (b) CJCSI 3511.01A (c) MCO 1001R.1L (d) ForO 4000.2A

Encl: (1) TRAINING EXERCISE AND EMPLOYMENT PLAN, TRANSPORTATION OF PERSONNEL,
TRANSPORTATION OF THINGS STANDING OPERATING PROCEDURES

1. Situation. Marine Forces Reserve (MARFORRES) units and designated Staff
sections will use the Training Exercise and Employment Plan, Transportation of
Personnel, Transportation of Things (T3) Program to identify units, personnel,
equipment and resources required to execute training exercises, deployments,
and other events in accordance with this Standing Operating Procedure (SOP)
and all references.

2. Cancellation. Force Order 3502.1 Ch 1

3. Mission. To establish policy, responsibilities and procedures for the
MARFORRES T3 Program and the use of the T3 database.

4. Execution

   a. Commander's Intent and Concept of Operations

      (1) Commander's Intent

         (a) Purpose. To provide commanders and MARFORRES Staff with a
comprehensive program for planning and scheduling training, transportation of
personnel, transportation of equipment and accounting for these costs in a
single database.

         (b) Method. The MARFORRES G-3/5 will update and maintain the T3
database and ensure that all training opportunities and resources are properly
annotated and available to the Force for tracking in accordance with this SOP.

         (c) End State. All training is properly planned, scheduled and
conducted with all resource costs captured.

      (2) Concept of Operations. The MARFORRES G-3/5 is responsible for the
operation and maintenance of the T3 database and provides oversight of the
MARFORRES Training Exercise and Employment Plan (TEEP). Major Subordinate
Commands (MSC) will input all training events into the T3 database and account
for all resources.

   b. Subordinate Element Missions

      (1) Assign sufficient personnel duties to ensure effective and efficient
use of T3.

      (2) Ensure personnel assigned T3 responsibilities complete computer
based training.

      (3) Adhere to the policies and procedures contained herein to plan and
execute unit training.

5. Administration and Logistics

   a. MARFORRES units and individuals activated in support of contingency
operations, combatant commander security cooperation deployments, or in
response to disasters or emergencies in the United States must submit requests
via T3, unless Defense Travel System (DTS) is authorized for transportation,
prior to transfer and upon return from the Gaining Force Command (GFC).
Preplanned and rotational activations are subject to the timelines in this
SOP. Emergent requirements will be dealt with as required for mission
accomplishment. Upon being designated as under Operational Control
(OPCON)/Administrative Control (ADCON) to the GFC, MARFORRES sourced units and
individual augmentees will be subject to the SOPs and directives of the GFC.

   b. This SOP is not applicable to Reserve and Active Component/Active
Reserve Marines and Sailors using DTS to schedule travel for a Non-TEEP funded
event.

   c. Recommendations concerning the content of this SOP should be submitted
via the chain of command to the AC/S G-3/5.

6. Command and Signal

   a. Command. This SOP is applicable to Marine Forces Reserve.

   b. Signal. This directive is effective the date signed.

B. S. JAMES

DISTRIBUTION STATEMENT A: Approved for public release; distribution is
unlimited.
