# ForO 3000-52.1, Chapter 1 — Introduction (verbatim)

## 1. Policy (p. 1-1)

The T3 database serves as a management tool for the Commander and is designed
to identify required units, personnel, equipment and other resources for the
efficient and effective execution of TEEP funded training exercises or events.
Additionally, the T3 is used to monitor events not utilizing TEEP funds; these
events are referred to as Non-TEEP funded events. Non-TEEP funded events
require the use of T3 database to account for transportation of personnel,
equipment and supplies. T3 is the only method of reserving commercial
transportation for reservists outside of DTS.

## 2. Scope (p. 1-1)

This SOP provides a comprehensive and iterative approach to T3 construction
and management. Managing and accounting for the funding of exercises and
training to include the movement of personnel, equipment and supplies is the
primary purpose of the T3 database. Funding is often the key factor in
determining the level of MARFORRES participation in exercises and training
events. For this reason, it is imperative that Training Officers/Chiefs take
their responsibilities seriously to ensure MARFORRES achieves the maximum
training value for each dollar. This SOP will cover both TEEP funded and
Non-TEEP funded events recorded for documentation and accountability in the T3
databases.

## 3. Responsibilities (pp. 1-1 to 1-4)

a. **Assistant Chief of Staff (AC/S), G-1** (p. 1-1): (1) Provide oversight
and subject matter expertise regarding the creation of Marine Resource Order
Writing Service (MROWS) and DTS Orders. (2) Establish procedures to ensure
administrators assigned across the force are trained to write and create MROWS
and DTS Orders correctly with proper funding data and T3 information. (3) As
required, provide a MROWS subject matter expert to conduct status of orders
review with the MARFORRES G-3/5, G-4, and Comptroller. (4) Provide subject
matter expertise as a voting member of the T3 Configuration Control Board (CCB).

b. **AC/S, G-3/5** (pp. 1-1 to 1-3): (1) Operate and maintain the MARFORRES T3
database. (2) Conduct an annual TEEP Conference with Major Subordinate
Commands (MSC) in order to determine funding for MARFORRES participation in
Joint, Service and MARFORRES required training and establish the basis for
funding controls for the MSCs. (3) Publish annual TEEP funding controls to the
MSCs. (4) Provide funding oversight of the MARFORRES TEEP. (5) Recommend
exercises and events to COMMARFORRES to be included in the TEEP,
based on mission analysis that highlights exercise objectives and training
benefits for MSC/MSEs participation as it relates to force generation model.
(6) Report the status of TEEP execution to COMMARFORRES, quarterly. (7) As
required, conduct status of funds review with the Comptroller and MARFORRES
G-4 to ensure TEEP expenditures remain within approved budget authorizations.
(8) Ensure funding for the TEEP is planned, programmed and budgeted via
coordination with the Command's Program Objective Memorandum (POM) Officer and
executed in accordance with the SOP. (9) Publish Exercise Directives for all
Combatant Commander, Service Level and MARFORRES sponsored exercises or events
that have multiple MSC participation. At a minimum, the Exercise Directives
will contain: (a) Exercise/Operation name. (b) Mission Essential Tasks (METs)
intended to be accomplished. (c) Concept of Operations for logistics, medical,
communications, etc. (d) Participating unit(s) and manning numbers. (e) Date
range to include any key dates, i.e. planning conferences and Post Deployment
System Support. (f) Command relationships. (g) Order writing guidance to
include date/location of movement requirements. (h) Orders funding guidance —
in the event the orders to be issued are supported by DTS and funded by
another command, this funding guidance will also provide DTS cross
organization funding and routing list requirements. (i) External support
required, i.e., contracting, environmental, etc. (10) Establish a T3 CCB to
review and approve system requirements, modifications, and other software
changes. Standing membership includes representatives from MARFORRES G-1,
G-3/5, G-4, and Comptroller and advisory representatives from IMKM and G-6 as
needed.

c. **AC/S, G-4** (p. 1-3): (1) Coordinate and schedule transportation
requirements for TEEP and Non-TEEP funded events when approved by the G-3/5 in
the T3 database. (2) Serve as the senior advisor for coordinating logistics in
the T3 database. (3) As required, provide a Distribution Management Office
(DMO) subject matter expert to conduct status of funds review with the
Comptroller and MARFORRES G-3/5. (4) Provide subject matter expertise as a
voting member of the T3 CCB.

d. **AC/S Comptroller** (p. 1-3): (1) Provide the MARFORRES G-3/5 with annual
TEEP Planning Estimates. (2) Assist the MARFORRES G-3/5 in Program oversight
of TEEP budgeting. (3) Provide assistance and guidance to the AC/S G-3 at the
MSCs on the application of Non-TEEP funding. (4) As required, provide a
Comptroller subject matter expert to conduct status of funds review with the
MARFORRES G-3/5 and G-4. (5) Provide subject matter expertise as a voting
member of the T3 CCB.

e. **AC/S G-3: 4th Marine Division, 4th Marine Aircraft Wing, 4th Marine
Logistics Group and Force Headquarters Group** (pp. 1-3 to 1-4): (1) Ensure
funding for the TEEP is planned, programmed and budgeted in coordination with
the AC/S G-3/5 and AC/S G-4 and executed in accordance with this SOP.
(2) Ensure expenditures remain within authorized TEEP and Non-TEEP controls.
(3) Forward all TEEP requests and subsequent changes to MARFORRES AC/S G-3/5
via the T3 officer for final approval. (4) Ensure all MROWS and DTS Orders are
created with the proper funding data and T3 information (IAW Chapter 7).
(5) Be prepared to provide a subject matter expert to the T3/TEEP status of
funds meeting. (6) Establish policy to ensure information entered in the T3
database is accurate, complete and up to date. (7) Provide oversight for your
units as they plan and execute TEEP funded events. (8) Publish Exercise
Directives for MSC sponsored exercises or events, containing at minimum the
information specified in paragraph 3.a.9. above. (9) Track and maintain Key
Supporting Documents for individual MSC transportation events.
